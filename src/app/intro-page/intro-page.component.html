Digital Signup for Epicodus Group Week

Dratfr is a tool for code school team week.

What can you do?

  • Add your idea to the dashboard
  • Team members sign up for group projects
  • Auto signin ability with Google or GitHub accounts
  • Leave comments for others' projects
  • View other courses' group projects
  • Sort projects by stage: idea-only, active, completed or all
  • Like projects
